Transport

A well-knit and coordinated system of transport plays an important role in the sustained growth of a country. Transport has recorded an extensive growth over the years both in spread of network and in output of the system. Glimpses of several modes of transport such as rail, road, water ways, air and metro are provided in this section. Details of the Ministries and organizations, policies, schemes, projects, online services related to the sector are given.
